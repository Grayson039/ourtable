# Our Table 🍽️

A household recipe and meal planning app. Save recipes from anywhere, scan your fridge, cook together.

## Live Prototype
**[View Prototype →](https://grayson039.github.io/ourtable/our-table-prototype.html)**

## What It Does
- Save recipes from any URL, social media (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), or manually
- Scan your fridge with your camera — app detects what you have and suggests meals
- Plan your week with the meal planner — assign recipes to any slot or auto-generate a full week
- Smart grocery list that groups items by aisle and syncs across your household
- Dietary & allergy profiles for every household member — app flags conflicts automatically
- Family Recipe Book — a digital heirloom cookbook with Party Mode and print/export
- Cooking Mode — step-by-step guided cooking with per-step ingredient callouts
