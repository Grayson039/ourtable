04 — Warm Slate + Saffron
The safest bet if you want versatility and broad appeal. Warm slate is professional without being clinical — it has enough warmth to work in a food context. Saffron is one of the most food-forward colors you can pick (literally named after a spice). Easy to read, works at every size. Lower risk, lower ceiling — won't surprise anyone but won't alienate anyone either.
